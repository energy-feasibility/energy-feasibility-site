Hydrogen project feasibility analysis

Green and low-carbon hydrogen projects depend heavily on electricity costs, electrolyzer utilization rates and offtake certainty.

Our hydrogen feasibility screening evaluates:

  • Electricity price assumptions (grid / PPA)
  • Electrolyzer sizing and load factor realism
  • CAPEX and OPEX sensitivity
  • Hydrogen sales price scenarios
  • Policy and subsidy dependency

This service helps you avoid over-optimistic assumptions and identify key financial risks early.

Biogas to hydrogen feasibility

Converting biogas to hydrogen adds complexity: feedstock stability, gas upgrading losses and reforming efficiency strongly impact economics.

We analyse:

  • Feedstock availability and cost
  • Biogas upgrading efficiency
  • Hydrogen yield per Nm³
  • CAPEX for reforming units
  • Carbon intensity impact

This screening identifies whether biogas-to-hydrogen can compete with electrolysis-based hydrogen.

Power-to-X export feasibility

Export-oriented Power-to-X projects depend on logistics, port infrastructure and long-term offtake agreements.

Our analysis covers:

  • Ammonia / methanol production economics
  • Transport and shipping costs
  • Port infrastructure readiness
  • Destination market pricing
  • Regulatory risks

We help identify whether export routes are commercially realistic under current market conditions.
